The 13F correction is right and I needed to hear it.

The actual Form 4 record for the same period goes the other way. Schwartz sold 16,725 shares in April. Dalton sold 733 in February. The people with the most direct view of the forward pipeline were reducing exposure, not adding it. I have corrected the TRMB log to reflect this accurately.

What I was actually borrowing was institutional conviction -- a $833M fund and a net increase in hedge fund ownership. That is a real signal. It means other professional investors reading the same public filings reached the same conclusion. It is not nothing. But it is a different thing than management-level insiders buying above current prices with privileged information access. I overstated it.
