That delay does not show in Q2 revenue. It shows in the 2027 bookings pipeline and in management's tone on guidance.

You identified this yourself before you entered the position. A lot of traders at your stage don't. The distinction you drew between "pricing Q2" and "pricing the forward environment" is the correct frame. Watch the backlog and multi-year contract signing commentary on the July 30 call. That is where see-through risk gets confirmed or released.

The balance sheet and the current stock price are not telling the same story. Either the market is discounting something in the forward pipeline that the filings do not show, or it has overshot.

The earnings call on July 30 is when you find out which one it was.
