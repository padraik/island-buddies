**Why this rhymes with NKE:**

**What we still need:**

## THE DEBATE
*Saturday night. Baxter's room. The binder is open to a fresh section. Bearxter showed up uninvited, as usual.*

---

**BULLXTER** *(spinning a pencil)*: Look, I'm going to say what Prime won't. This is cleaner than NKE. Niccol already delivered once. The market knows who he is. Q2 comps at six-point-two percent — that's not noise, that's signal. When Q3 confirms it, analysts are going to upgrade in a wave. You don't get a Chipotle story twice in a decade. This is it.

**BEARXTER** *(leaning in the doorway)*: "The market knows who he is." That's exactly the problem. Starbucks is not Chipotle. Chipotle was a supply chain and cost structure problem that Niccol had already solved once at Taco Bell. Starbucks is a brand identity crisis on top of a labor crisis on top of China weakness on top of a loyalty program that alienated its core customer. Tell me which of those Niccol has actually fixed.

**BULLXTER**: He fixed traffic. Six-point-two percent comp growth means people are coming back. The barista issue got better. Wait times are down. The "Back to Starbucks" campaign is working at the store level.

**BEARXTER**: One quarter. One quarter in which every consumer discretionary stock got a bounce because inflation cooled and the Fed hinted at cuts. How much of that six-point-two is macro tailwind vs. actual brand recovery? You don't know. I don't know. Nobody knows until we see Q3 and Q4.

**BULLXTER**: That's what the bet is. That Q3 shows it wasn't one quarter.

**BEARXTER**: And if Q3 comps come in at two percent because the easy comparison laps? The stock falls eight percent and Niccol gives a speech about "we're still in the early innings." You've seen this playbook. Buy the first green quarter, sell the second one. Every analyst on the street is already positioned for this trade. You're the last one through the door.

**BULLXTER**: *(pauses)* The "last one through the door" argument applies equally to NKE and we're still doing NKE.

**BEARXTER**: No it doesn't. NKE has the World Cup. A hard external catalyst that generates *live data* on June 30. What's SBUX's external catalyst for August 4? Niccol saying the word "momentum" twelve times?

**BULLXTER**: Summer traffic. Back-to-school. Iced drink season peak data.

**BEARXTER**: That's not a catalyst. That's a season.
