Starbucks at $78 is a turnaround bet. 

Starbucks at $99 is a turnaround that already happened.

The distance from $78 (52-week low) to $99 (today) is **+27%**. That's the Niccol premium. The market already ran the trade. What's left is 8% to analyst consensus — on a stock where options are priced for a company with a $99 stock price.

NKE works because the stock is still cheap. SBUX doesn't work because it isn't anymore.
