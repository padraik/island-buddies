## THE HOUSING MACRO — HONEST ASSESSMENT

**Headwinds (real, don't ignore):**
- 30-year mortgage rates staying above 6% through 2026 (Fed not cutting further)
- 60% of builders using sales incentives — 13th consecutive month
- 36% cutting prices in April 2026
- Affordability crisis: first-time buyers priced out in many markets
- KBH specifically targets first-time buyers — most rate-sensitive segment

**Tailwinds:**
- U.S. housing shortage: millions of units below equilibrium — structural demand exists
- Demographic tailwind: Millennials aging into home-buying years
- KB Home "Built-to-Order" model means no spec inventory risk (build when you have a signed buyer)
- Rate sensitivity: any rate relief, even from 6.5% to 6.0%, dramatically improves affordability
- New home buyers get builder incentives (rate buydowns, closing cost credits) that existing home market can't match
