## GLOSSARY

**Revenue per available seat mile (RASM):** The primary airline revenue productivity metric. Total revenue divided by available seat miles (ASMs = seats × miles flown). If RASM is growing, airlines are either filling more seats or charging higher prices per seat. Management guides this metric specifically on earnings calls. A RASM guide below consensus is the primary catalyst for airline stock selloffs.

**Available seat mile (ASM):** One seat flown one mile. Multiply seats × route miles × flights = total capacity. Airlines report this quarterly. RASM = Revenue / ASM. Cost per ASM (CASM) is the cost side.

**Gross profit per unit (GPU):** Carvana's primary profitability metric. For each used car sold, GPU = sale price minus acquisition cost minus reconditioning cost minus transportation. A normalizing used car market compresses GPU because CVNA must pay more to acquire vehicles (competitive auctions) but can't raise selling prices (consumer price sensitivity). Target GPU ~$3,000-3,500.

**Manheim Used Vehicle Value Index:** The industry benchmark for wholesale used car prices. Published monthly by Manheim/Cox Automotive. If the index is declining, used car prices are falling, and CVNA's GPU compresses mechanically. This is the macro data series that drives CVNA's earnings fundamentals.

**Fuel hedge:** Airlines pre-purchase fuel at fixed prices for future delivery using derivatives. A hedge that was set 6-12 months ago at lower prices provides cost certainty. When that hedge rolls off and is replaced at current (higher) prices, fuel costs spike. If Iran conflict drove crude higher in Q1-Q2 2026, airlines' Q3-Q4 hedge roll-off could be costly.

**Macau VIP gaming:** High-stakes gambling at Macau casinos, traditionally dominated by Chinese high-net-worth individuals and organized junket operators. VIP gaming revenue is extremely volatile (one whale can move quarterly results) and has been recovering since 2023 after COVID lockdowns. WYNN Cotai is one of the premier VIP properties. VIP volumes are sensitive to Chinese economic confidence.
