## GLOSSARY

**Tesla Q2 delivery report:** Tesla releases vehicle delivery and production data quarterly, typically at the end of the first week following quarter-end. Q2 ends June 30; delivery report likely June 30 - July 7. This data is released BEFORE the full earnings call (approximately July 22-23). The delivery number moves the stock significantly.

**Automotive gross margin:** Tesla's profit on vehicle sales after direct costs (materials, labor, manufacturing). Excludes software, services, and energy. Consensus for Q2 2026 is ~14-15%. Any print below 13% historically drops the stock 10-20% in one session.

**Full Self-Driving (FSD):** Tesla's driver-assist/autonomous driving software package. Priced at $8,000-$12,000. Revenue recognition is deferred; actual contribution to margins is complex. Bears argue FSD is vaporware at commercial scale; bulls argue it's the most undervalued asset in the company.

**Cybercab / Robotaxi:** Tesla's planned autonomous electric taxi. Originally announced for 2024, then 2025, then Q4 2026. Each delay has historically been followed by a Elon announcement of the NEXT timeline. At $405, the market is pricing in a near-term robotaxi success story.

**DOGE (Department of Government Efficiency):** Advisory body led by Elon Musk under the Trump administration. Elon's time at DOGE reduces operational attention to Tesla. Tesla customer base (left-leaning, environmentally motivated) has expressed concern about brand damage from political association. Both effects are bearish for TSLA.

**Black-Scholes estimate:** A theoretical options pricing model using stock price, strike price, time to expiry, risk-free rate, and implied volatility. The estimates in this file use TSLA IV ~65% based on inferred IV from the live call chain. Actual IV may differ; live chain verification required.

**Put breakeven:** Strike price minus premium paid. For $250P at $1.00: breakeven = $249. Stock must close BELOW $249 at Aug 21 expiry to generate profit.
