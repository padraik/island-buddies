## GLOSSARY

**Connected TV (CTV):** Television content streamed via internet-connected devices (Roku, Fire TV, smart TVs). CTV advertising is distinct from traditional linear TV and from digital display advertising. The Trade Desk's thesis is that programmatic ad buying (automated, real-time bidding) will dominate CTV the way it dominates desktop/mobile display. CTV advertisers pay premium CPMs for the targeting precision that traditional TV couldn't offer.

**Unified ID 2.0 (UID2):** The Trade Desk's alternative to browser cookies for tracking digital ad audiences. As Chrome phases out third-party cookies, UID2 uses email-based hashing to enable cross-site tracking with user consent. TTD's long-term moat depends partly on UID2 adoption becoming the industry standard. Delays in cookie deprecation reduce the urgency of UID2 adoption.

**Same-store sales comp:** Year-over-year percentage change in revenue from stores/locations open for at least one year. Filters out growth from new openings. The primary indicator of brand health and pricing power in consumer restaurants (CMG, SBUX) and retail (TGT, WMT).

**Ticket size:** Average transaction value per customer visit. At CMG and SBUX, pricing power is measured by whether ticket size grows YoY (pricing increases passing through) or declines (discounting or smaller portions ordered). If ticket size can no longer grow, same-store comp depends entirely on traffic — which is harder to control.

**Float:** The number of shares actually available for public trading, excluding shares held by insiders, major institutions with lockup agreements, and the company itself. Short interest as a percentage of float is the correct squeeze risk metric (not shares outstanding).

**Programmatic advertising:** Automated buying and selling of online advertising in real-time auctions. The advertiser submits a bid through a demand-side platform (DSP) like The Trade Desk; the publisher's supply-side platform accepts the winning bid; the ad displays in milliseconds. CPMs (cost per thousand impressions) vary by audience quality and targeting precision.
