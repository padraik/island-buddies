## HOW MONITORING WORKS

Every pass gets an initial decision at entry. Every Monday, each active entry gets one of three outcomes:

- **Keep Watching** -- thesis still intact, catalyst not yet resolved, nothing has changed the decision
- **Stop Watching** -- catalyst window closed, thesis invalidated, or stock moved so far that the setup is gone
- **Create New Pitch** -- something changed that removes the original pass reason; Baxter builds a full research document for the new week

Each entry lists the specific conditions for "Create New Pitch" and "Stop Watching" upfront, so Monday is a check against known criteria -- not a re-argument of the whole thesis.
