### Three Stages

**STAGE 1 — FOUNDATION ($200 → $5,000)**
- Prove the system works. Capital is scarce. Every play matters.
- Max position size: 50% of fund
- Target per-play: 3-5x premium
- Acceptable loss: full premium (100% of position)
- Play types: ATM/1-OTM calls on earnings catalysts, 3-6 week timing
- Options cost target: $0.50 - $2.50/contract (keeps us in 1-3 contracts)

**STAGE 2 — GROWTH ($5,000 → $500,000)**
- Scale up position sizes. Can afford more research time per play.
- Max position size: 40% of fund
- Target per-play: 2-3x premium
- Play types: Earnings + momentum breakouts + sector catalyst plays
- Can add LEAPS for longer thesis plays

**STAGE 3 — TERMINAL ($500,000 → $5,000,000)**
- Protect capital. One bad play doesn't end the island.
- Max position size: 25% of fund
- Target per-play: 1.5-2x premium
- Play types: Blue chip earnings, sector ETF options, dividend plays alongside
- Preserve toward the island math: $2.1M invested at 4.5% = $94,500/yr

### The Iron Rules

1. **Never risk more than 50% of total fund on a single play.** Ever.
2. **If the fund drops below 40% of its all-time high, stop and reassess.** No revenge trading.
3. **Never average down on a losing position.** If the thesis broke, exit.
4. **Take profits at target.** Don't get greedy chasing 10x when 3x was the plan.
5. **No plays with earnings in less than 10 days.** Too little time for thesis to play out.
