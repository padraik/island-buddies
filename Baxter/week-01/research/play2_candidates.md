## WHAT WE DON'T KNOW YET

**The important question Bearxter will ask:**

*"If the business is growing 17% and heading toward profitability, why is the stock down 48% from its high?"*

This needs an answer before this is a real pitch. The possibilities:
1. Competition is intensifying (FanDuel, ESPN Bet, Fanatics Bet, BetMGM all fighting for the same customers)
2. Marketing costs are too high — customer acquisition economics are deteriorating
3. The market re-rated the entire sports betting sector lower (multiple compression)
4. Something specific happened that spooked investors

If the answer is #3 (sector re-rate) and the underlying business is healthy — that's NKE in Q4 2024 all over again. The stock fell because sentiment turned, not because the business broke. When sentiment reverses, it moves fast.

If the answer is #1 or #2 — the thesis is harder.

**Research agenda for the next session:**
1. Why did DKNG fall from $48 to $25? What happened?
2. DKNG historical earnings reactions — does it beat-then-fall?
3. Market share data — is DraftKings winning or losing the customer acquisition war?
4. Options chain estimate — what does a $28C or $30C Aug 15 look like at current prices?
