## THE BEARXTER DISCOVERY

*The short thesis on Disney has been the same for five years. Streaming burns cash. Cord-cutting destroys the linear TV business. ESPN faces an existential transition. Bob Iger can't run the company forever and there's no obvious successor. The stock has reflected this thesis -- from a two-hundred-dollar peak in 2021, through the streaming buildout, through the Iger return, through the activist pressure, down to a fifty-two week low of ninety-two dollars.*

*Bearxter had been watching the streaming thesis specifically. Not because he thought streaming would work -- he's skeptical of every positive thesis. He was watching because a thesis that is wrong by a measurable amount eventually becomes a trade.*

*On May 6, 2026, Disney reported Q2 fiscal 2026 earnings. Entertainment streaming SVOD revenue: five-point-five billion dollars, up thirteen percent. SVOD operating income: five-hundred-and-eighty-two million dollars, up eighty-eight percent. SVOD operating margin: ten-point-six percent.*

*First double-digit streaming margin in Disney's history.*

*Management confirmed they remain on track for at least ten percent full-year SVOD margin.*

*The streaming bear thesis is factually over. The stock went up eight percent on May 6 and settled at ninety-nine dollars. The fifty-two week range is ninety-two to one-hundred-twenty-four. Disney is at twenty-one percent of its range. The market has partially processed the information. It has not fully processed it.*

*Bearxter brought it in.*

## GLOSSARY

**SVOD (Subscription Video On Demand):** Streaming subscriptions like Disney+. Distinct from AVOD (ad-supported video) or PVOD (premium video on demand). When Disney reports streaming profitability, they're reporting the SVOD operating margin -- revenue from subscriptions minus content costs, technology, marketing, and overhead.

**SVOD operating margin:** The percentage of SVOD revenue retained as operating profit. Disney's 10.6% SVOD operating margin in Q2 FY2026 means that for every $100 in streaming subscription revenue, Disney kept $10.60 as operating income. Netflix operates at approximately 29% operating margin. Disney reaching double-digits is the inflection point.

**Q2 FY2026:** Disney's fiscal year runs October through September. Q2 FY2026 covers January through March 2026, reported May 6, 2026. This is the quarter that showed the first double-digit streaming margin.

**Q3 FY2026:** Covers April through June 2026. Expected to be reported in August 2026. This is the summer season -- peak parks attendance, summer film slate. Management guided Q3 total segment operating income of approximately $5.3 billion.

**Cord-cutting:** When cable TV subscribers cancel their subscription and shift to streaming services. Linear TV channels (ESPN, ABC, Disney Channel) lose affiliate fees as fewer households pay for cable. Disney's response: move ESPN and its content directly to streaming consumers via the Flagship ESPN app (direct-to-consumer) and the Disney+/Hulu bundle.

**Josh D'Amaro:** Chairman of Disney Experiences, the segment that includes theme parks, cruise lines, and consumer products. The parks segment has been the most consistently profitable part of Disney's business for several years. D'Amaro's reported candidacy as Iger's successor reflects the financial importance of the parks to Disney's overall results.

**CEO succession overhang:** When a company's chief executive has announced they will leave or is approaching the end of their tenure, the stock often trades at a discount reflecting the uncertainty around future leadership. Disney's overhang has been particularly pronounced because Bob Iger (who originally retired and was brought back) has an end-of-2026 contract, making the timeline definitive but the candidate uncertain.

**Disney+ / Hulu bundle:** Disney's combined streaming offering. Disney+ for family and franchise content (Marvel, Star Wars, Pixar), Hulu for general entertainment and live TV. The bundle is the core vehicle for Disney's streaming subscriber growth and the platform for the Flagship ESPN app.
