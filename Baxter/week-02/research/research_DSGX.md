## GLOSSARY

**Logistics software:** Technology that manages the planning, execution, and optimization of the movement and storage of goods. Includes customs compliance, route optimization, freight management, and regulatory filing. Descartes specializes in the compliance and customs piece, which is directly driven by trade policy complexity.

**FCF (Free Cash Flow):** Operating cash flow minus capital expenditures. Measures the cash a business generates after maintaining its operations. A company with $260M in FCF on $729M revenue is converting 35.7% of revenue to free cash -- that's high-quality business operation.

**mNAV:** Not applicable.

**Bear target:** The most pessimistic 12-month price target among analysts covering the stock. For DSGX, the official lowest is $82 though recent cuts have landed at $90, suggesting the $82 may be from a target that has not been recently revised.
