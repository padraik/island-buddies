## THE IRON RULES (CALLS) — condensed

All five must pass. One fail kills the play.

1. **Stock in bottom 20-25% of 52-week range.** Price dislocation is the entry signal.
2. **Confirmed earnings catalyst before expiry.** Earnings forces resolution. Everything else is narrative.
3. **Near-zero Sell ratings.** Zero or one Underperform in the covering universe.
4. **Bear floor above breakeven.** Lowest Buy target must be above (strike + premium). If it drops below after entry: **exit same day. No waiting.**
5. **Option ask at or below $1.00.** Chain quality gate. Not waived for a good thesis.

---

## EXIT PROTOCOLS

- **Standard:** Sell at market open the morning after earnings. Thesis resolved. Done.
- **Rule 4 live breach:** Exit same day. No exceptions. This rule was written in blood after BSX.
- **BOTZ watch:** If no progress toward breakeven within two weeks of expiry, sell into remaining time value.
- **Pre-earnings profit target:** If stock reaches the bear floor price *before* earnings, consider same-day exit. Thesis resolved early.
- **Never:** Average down. Hold past the morning after earnings. Waive a Rule 4 breach.
