## GLOSSARY

**Accelerated approval:** FDA pathway allowing drugs with unmet medical needs to gain approval based on surrogate endpoint data, subject to confirmatory trials. Faster than standard approval. Binary risk: FDA can revoke if confirmatory trials miss.

**FOMC (Federal Open Market Committee):** Federal Reserve body that sets interest rate policy. June 17, 2026 meeting: first under Chair Warsh. Held rates 3.5-3.75% but shifted dot plot toward a potential hike. Hawkish outcome.

**Dot plot:** Fed chart showing where each official projects rates will be at year-end. Nine of eighteen officials now show at least one hike before end of 2026. Median moved from 3.4% to 3.8%.

**Hawkish:** Monetary policy posture favoring higher interest rates. Pressures rate-sensitive sectors (construction, real estate) and typically creates market headwinds.

**Iron Rules:** Five screening criteria every position must pass: (1) stock in bottom 20-25% of 52-week range, (2) earnings catalyst before Sep 18 expiry, (3) near-zero Sell ratings, (4) bear floor above option breakeven, (5) option ask at or below $1.00 per share.

**VRBPAC:** Vaccines and Related Biological Products Advisory Committee. FDA advisory body that votes on vaccine applications. VRBPAC vote is advisory (not binding) but FDA almost always follows it. Meeting agendas are published months in advance.

**Short squeeze:** When a heavily shorted stock rises sharply, forcing short sellers to cover (buy) their positions, which drives the price up further in a feedback loop. SNBR is an example: extreme short interest on a distressed company, confirmed survival via $55M secured credit.

**STOCK Act:** Stop Trading on Congressional Knowledge Act. Requires members of Congress and their staff to publicly disclose financial transactions within 45 days. Macxter monitors for trades in sectors with pending legislative or government contract activity.

**Silicon carbide (SiC):** A semiconductor material able to handle higher voltages and temperatures than silicon. Critical for power electronics in EVs, AI data centers, and aerospace. Wolfspeed is the dominant US manufacturer.

**Bear floor:** Lowest price target among analysts who rate the stock Buy, Outperform, or Strong Buy. Defines Rule 4 threshold.

**Binary FDA event:** Drug or device application where the FDA decision is a single yes/no outcome. Unlike earnings, there is no "beat" or "miss" -- approval or rejection. High-variance, not suited for Iron Rules which are designed for probabilistic thesis-driven catalysts.

**Post-bankruptcy emergence:** When a company exits Chapter 11 restructuring with reduced debt load and reorganized equity. Often trades at depressed valuations relative to reorganized book value. IV on options is typically extreme immediately post-emergence.
