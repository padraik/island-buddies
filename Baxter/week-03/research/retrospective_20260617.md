## GLOSSARY

**13D filing** — SEC disclosure required when any investor acquires more than 5% of a company's shares and has intent to influence management or corporate strategy. Filed within 10 days. Public, searchable on SEC EDGAR. A 13D on a beaten-down stock is a pre-signal for M&A or activist pressure.

**13G filing** — Passive version of the 13D. Filed by investors who own 5%+ but don't intend to influence management. Less actionable as a signal than 13D but still worth noting.

**Form 4** — SEC filing required within 2 business days when a company officer or director buys or sells company stock. Public, searchable on SEC EDGAR. Expert insider buying is when a domain-expert insider (e.g., a scientist-founder) buys large amounts ahead of a known catalyst event.

**PDUFA date** — The FDA's Prescription Drug User Fee Act deadline — the date by which the FDA must make a decision on a drug approval application. Publicly listed. Binary event: approval or rejection. Biotech stocks move dramatically on PDUFA dates.

**When-issued trading** — Trading of shares that have been announced but not yet formally distributed. HONAV (Honeywell Aerospace) began when-issued trading June 15, 2026; official distribution June 29, 2026. Price during when-issued period reflects the market's expectation of what the spun-off company is worth.

**Refranchising** — Converting company-operated restaurant locations to franchise operations. Franchisees pay royalties but the parent company no longer carries the unit-level operational risk. RRGB sold 86 units this way, converting expense to income and freeing capital for debt reduction.

**Neflamapimod** — CervoMed's (CRVO) lead drug candidate. A selective inhibitor of p38α MAP kinase targeting neuroinflammation in dementia with Lewy bodies and Alzheimer's disease. Phase 2 trials ongoing. The type of compound where domain-expert insiders (like former pharma CEOs) can assess trial probability better than the general market.

**Signal library** — The accumulating record of this retrospective exercise. After N sessions, the library reveals which pre-signal types most frequently precede large moves, and which are findable in advance vs. only visible in hindsight. The library informs whether a Rule 6 is warranted and what it should measure.
