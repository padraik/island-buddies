Their tech is in Hyundai, Stellantis, and a growing list of restaurant chains. They are not a chatbot company; they own their own speech stack end to end instead of renting one. They are not yet profitable — losing roughly $25M a quarter — and that is the entire fight over this stock.

## GLOSSARY

- **All-stock acquisition:** Buying a company by issuing new shares instead of paying cash. Existing shareholders own a smaller slice afterward — that's dilution — which is why the market sold the April announcement.
- **Dilution:** Increase in share count that shrinks each existing share's claim on the business.
- **52-week range percentile:** Where the price sits between its 1-year low (0%) and high (100%). Rule 1's sorting key; SOUN is at 4.9%.
- **Breakeven:** Strike + premium paid. $7 + $0.63 = $7.63; the stock price where the call pays for itself at expiry.
- **Straddle / implied move:** A call and put at the same strike and expiry; their combined price is the market's estimate of the total move through the event. Here ~25%.
- **IV ramp / IV crush:** Implied volatility inflating into a known event (ramp) and collapsing the moment it passes (crush). Tab 4 sells the ramp and refuses the crush.
- **Short interest / days to cover:** Shares sold short as % of float (37.9-40.2%), and days of average volume needed to buy them back (4.87). The squeeze-fuel gauges.
- **Short squeeze:** Forced buying by shorts covering losses as a stock rises, accelerating the rise. How far-OTM calls actually get paid inside six weeks.
- **Form 4 / RSU tax-withholding sale:** Insider transaction filing / a mechanical sale of vested stock to cover taxes. Not a discretionary signal.
- **Estimate revisions:** Direction consensus EPS forecasts moved recently. Falling revisions into a print raise miss risk. The one UNVERIFIED line in this doc.
- **EV (expected value):** Probability-weighted average outcome across scenarios. Positive EV means the play makes money on average, not that it makes money this time.
- **DTE:** Days to expiration.
- **Bear floor (Rule 4):** The lowest Buy-rated analyst target, which must exceed breakeven — here $12 vs $7.63.
- **2-open-3.5/5 cap:** House rule limiting simultaneous open 3.5/5-conviction positions to two (currently TRMB, LYFT). A 3.5/5 SOUN would be queued, not entered.
