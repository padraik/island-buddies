**The Setup:** Same macro driver as OXY (Hormuz + war + hot CPI energy print) but XOM is lower-beta — diversified across upstream, downstream, chemicals. So it moves less per barrel. Aug21 $165C is ~$340 (OI 4,254, the most liquid strike), $160C ~$460. The problem: at $150 spot, the affordable strikes ($165) are ~10% OTM, so we need a real move to profit. The closer strikes ($150/$155 ATM) cost $625–$830 — over budget for a single contract.

**What Could Go Wrong:** Lower beta cuts both ways — if oil rips, XOM lags OXY; if oil fades on a ceasefire, XOM holds up better but our OTM calls still bleed theta. The affordable strikes are too far OTM to be efficient. Honestly OXY is the better expression of the identical thesis on our budget.
