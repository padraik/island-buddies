## GLOSSARY
- **Breakeven:** Strike + premium paid; the stock price the option needs to reach for you to recover your cost ($60.73 here).
- **Guidance cut:** Management lowering its own forward revenue/EPS outlook — a structural negative; a second one would confirm the decline isn't temporary.
- **Debit spread:** Buying one call and selling a higher-strike call to cut cost and define risk — a cheaper, lower-breakeven way to play a recovery.
- **WATCHMAN / EP:** BSX's flagship cardiology device / electrophysiology segment — the products whose slowdown drove the April guidance cut.
