## GLOSSARY
- **Dot plot:** The Fed's published chart of members' future rate projections; removing it strips the market of forward guidance.
- **Hedge / put:** A long put gains value if the market falls, offsetting losses on our long-call positions — portfolio insurance.
- **VIX / realized vol:** VIX is the market's expected volatility; realized vol is what actually happened. VIX above realized = options pricing an event premium.
