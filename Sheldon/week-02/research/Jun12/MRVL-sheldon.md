## GLOSSARY
- **Index inclusion / forced buying:** When a stock joins the S&P 500, every S&P 500 index fund must buy it to track the index — creating guaranteed demand on the effective date.
- **IV (implied volatility):** How expensive options are relative to expected movement. 100% IV = the market expects HUGE swings and you pay through the nose.
- **Sell the news:** When a catalyst is so anticipated that buyers exhaust themselves before it lands, and the stock falls on the actual event.
