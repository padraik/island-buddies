## GLOSSARY
- **Theta:** Time decay. Every day an option loses a little value as expiry approaches — brutal if your catalyst is far away.
- **Data mechanism:** A dated event (earnings, report) that forces the market to re-price the thesis. No mechanism before expiry = no reason for the stock to move your way in time.
- **BOTZ rule:** Our hard-won lesson — a theme without a dated catalyst before expiry is dead money, no matter how good the story.
