## GLOSSARY
- **P/E multiple (45x):** Price divided by earnings; high multiples price in growth and fall hard when sentiment sours.
- **Multiple compression:** When a stock's P/E shrinks (often on rate fears), dragging the price down even if earnings hold.
- **Dividend yield as cushion:** A higher yield supports a stock in a selloff; WMT's 0.73% offers almost none.
