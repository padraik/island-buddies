## GLOSSARY
- **Ex-dividend date:** The day a stock starts trading without its next dividend; the price drops ~the dividend amount, pressuring calls.
- **Dot plot:** The Fed's chart of members' rate projections; eliminating it removes forward guidance and increases post-meeting uncertainty.
- **IV crush:** Implied volatility collapsing after a known event, deflating option premiums.
