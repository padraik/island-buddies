## GLOSSARY
- **Shiller CAPE (CAPE 40.43)**: cyclically-adjusted P/E using 10yr avg earnings. 40+ is dot-com-bubble territory; historically precedes weak forward decade returns.
- **Defensive / low-beta**: moves less than the market; holds up better in selloffs.
- **Shares-only**: no liquid options market, so you can't build a capped-risk spread or buy a protective put — you own the stock outright.
