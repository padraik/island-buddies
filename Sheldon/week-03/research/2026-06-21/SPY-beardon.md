## GLOSSARY
- **Shiller CAPE (40.43)**: price ÷ 10yr inflation-adjusted avg earnings. Smooths out cycles. 40+ = extreme; matched only near the 2000 peak.
- **VIX (16.78)**: 30-day expected S&P volatility ("fear gauge"). Below ~17 = complacency; cheap to buy protection, which usually means nobody is.
- **Cash as a hedge**: holding cash means market drops don't touch that portion AND (at 3.5% rates) it earns yield while you wait. The poor man's put.
