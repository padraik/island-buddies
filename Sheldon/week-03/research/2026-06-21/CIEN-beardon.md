## GLOSSARY
- **GF Value**: GuruFocus's intrinsic-value estimate. CIEN trading ~384% above it = extreme overvaluation flag.
- **Book-to-bill**: ratio of orders received to orders filled; >1 means demand is outrunning shipments (bullish backlog).
- **Beat-and-fade**: stock falls despite beating earnings, usually because guidance or valuation disappoints. CIEN did this Jun 4, then recovered.
- **CAPE-style valuation short**: betting against a stock purely because it's expensive — historically a terrible standalone timing signal.
