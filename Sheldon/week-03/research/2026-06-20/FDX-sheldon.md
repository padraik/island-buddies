## GLOSSARY
- **Spinoff (FDXF):** FedEx separated its Freight (LTL trucking) division into a new standalone NYSE company on Jun 1, 2026. The remaining FDX is now express/ground — but it makes year-over-year comparisons confusing for a quarter or two.
- **Bellwether:** A stock whose results signal the health of the broader economy. FDX shipping volumes = real-time global trade gauge.
